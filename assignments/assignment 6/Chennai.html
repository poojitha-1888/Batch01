Chennai

Chennai, formerly known as Madras, is the vibrant capital of Tamil Nadu, located on the Coromandel Coast of the Bay of Bengal. As a major commercial, cultural, and educational hub, it is renowned as the "Cultural Capital of South India" and the "Detroit of India" for its massive automobile industry. The city blends deep-rooted traditions with modern metropolis life, featuring historic temples, British-era architecture, and expansive beaches.

Chennai is popularly known as the "Gateway to South India," the "Cultural Capital of South India," and the "Detroit of India," due to its significance as a cultural hub, major port, and automotive manufacturing center, also recognized for its many temples and its former name, Madras.
